History of the School Kids’ Love Nursery and day care center started in this academic year 2018. This is a brain child of 2 directors with an education background and much experience in the education system of Uganda. Due to the challenges in many nursery schools, their main target is to provide a step towards bettering the kind of education in Uganda and particularly Nakasozi, Buddo. Their aim of starting with Nursery and Day care is due to the fact that early kids education is the foundation of all levels of education like socialization, character development extra.

  • motto: Carry your candle and light the world.
  • mission: To provide Holistic kind of Education
  • vision: Developing a God fearing pupil equipped with Quality education for the development of the nation

Structure of classes

The school has 3 classes i.e. Baby class, Middle class and Top class and for kids to be placed in Top class, he/she is subjected to a Top class test and upon fulfilling the requirements as set by the school then the pupil is placed in Top class. Attendance of 2 years programme. The school has special arrangements with those parents who feel their kids cannot spend 3 years studying nursery e.g. Baby, middle and top so that the kid studies from Baby to Top or middle to top. However, this kind of arrangement depends on the discussions between parents and management although the final decision to allow or reject a kid from skipping a level is discretional to the management. Structures; The school has one classroom block that houses the office, baby class, middle class, top class and the resting room. The school as well has well improved pit latrine however, in future we plan to construct a modern toilet facility with flashing toilets but at the moment we are back held by low funds.

Why should we be trusted to educate the kids?

The curriculum is based on the kid’s age and developmental readiness. kids get what they need for emotional, social, language and physical development.
  • Kids are never bored at kids love setting as it is in home setting. There is always something to do and kids interact; kids learn to socialize with other kids and learn to navigate the challenges of peer relationships. They learn self – control, how to get along with others and how to share with others.

We take pride in our clean, state of the art facilities. Our teachers are trained in infectious disease prevention and to understand the best methods to ensure the health and safety of each kid from infant kids care through preschool.
  • To ensure our environment is safe, we employ a variety of methods to keep our kids’ health. Like teaching them good health practical habits.
  • All meals and snacks are prepared at commercial grade kitchen away from classrooms.
  • Kids are served and eat in a place prepared specifically for meal hours.
  • Class Rooms are provided with checklists so that teachers can ensure each class room is clean and tidy.

  • We have a metal detector machine to check who ever enters Kids Love.
  • Kids issue cards containing the parent’s contacts and students’ passport photo.
  • We train kids to master the parent’s numbers and the class teacher’s numbers.
